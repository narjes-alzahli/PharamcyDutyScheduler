PROJECT OVERVIEW
================
An AI-powered staff scheduling system designed specifically for hospital pharmacy 
departments to optimize workforce management, ensure fair shift distribution, and 
maintain optimal patient care coverage.

THE CHALLENGE
=============
Hospital pharmacy departments face complex scheduling challenges:
• Manual roster creation is time-consuming and error-prone
• Balancing staff preferences with operational requirements
• Ensuring fair workload distribution across all staff
• Managing complex constraints (skills, availability, regulations)
• Maintaining adequate coverage during peak hours and holidays
• Compliance with labor laws and union agreements

OUR SOLUTION
============
An intelligent rostering system that automatically generates optimal monthly 
schedules using advanced constraint programming and AI optimization.

KEY FEATURES
============
🎯 AUTOMATED SCHEDULING
   • Generates complete monthly rosters in minutes
   • Handles 25+ staff members with complex constraints
   • Supports multiple shift types (Main, Outpatient, Inpatient, Evening, Night)
   • Manages leave requests, time-off, and special assignments

⚖️ FAIRNESS & BALANCE
   • Ensures equitable workload distribution
   • Prevents over-scheduling of individual staff
   • Balances desirable and undesirable shifts
   • Maintains work-life balance for all employees

📊 INTELLIGENT OPTIMIZATION
   • Advanced constraint programming (OR-Tools CP-SAT)
   • Multi-objective optimization (coverage, fairness, preferences)
   • Real-time conflict resolution
   • Penalty-based system for constraint violations

🎨 PROFESSIONAL VISUALIZATION
   • Color-coded schedule tables matching hospital standards
   • Interactive web interface for easy management
   • Real-time schedule editing and updates
   • Comprehensive reporting and analytics

🔧 FLEXIBLE CONFIGURATION
   • Customizable staff skills and qualifications
   • Adjustable coverage requirements
   • Configurable penalty weights
   • Support for hospital-specific rules and regulations

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
========================
• Built with Python 3.11 and modern web technologies
• Uses Google OR-Tools for optimization engine
